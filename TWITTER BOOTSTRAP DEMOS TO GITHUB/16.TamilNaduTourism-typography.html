Tour of Tamil Nadu- The Land of Vedas.

Tamil Nadu, literally The Land of Tamils is one of the 29 states of India. The capital of Tamil Nadu is Chennai.

-Author Name

Location

Located in south India, TN is bound by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka in the northern side, Indian Ocean in the southern side, Bay of Bengal in the eastern side and Kerala in the western side.

Fairs & Festivals

Pongal is the main festival in Tamil Nadu. Pongal is a harvest festival which is celebrated in the month of January. Feasting, music and dance mark this festival. Thaipusam, yet another celebration which comes in months of January and February in Thanjavur and Palani, is marked by ritual bathing.
